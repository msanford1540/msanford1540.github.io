Privacy Policy

Tic-Tac-Spatial does not collect, share, or sell any user infomation. Tic-Tac-Spatial does not interact with any remote servers other than Apple's.
Users who have opted into Share Analytics with App Developers in the Privacy tab in Settings should refer to the Apple Privacy Policy. Analytics provided to Tic-Tac-Spatial developers from this mechanism are not shared with outside parties.
